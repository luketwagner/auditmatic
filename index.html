Words that linger

Agentic AI

The cursor blinks. You type a wish. The agent reads, then starts to build—

Morning Light

First gold across the sill, the room still cool and half asleep.

The Sea at Dusk

The waves fold in with long blue sighs, the gulls go home, the pier lights rise.

In the Garden

Between the rows of green and stone, the bees know more than we have shown.

Memory

Not the whole day—a slant of sun, a phrase you said, and then it’s gone.

Debugging

One line was wrong. The screen went red. You fixed the bug and went to bed.
